About Me

Maj Straus

Who am I?

I am a 27 year old multimedia producer, born in Ljubljana, Slovenia. After finishing high school I studied multimedia production, primarely audio production at IAM (Institute & Academy of Multimedia) where I graduated in october 2017. My biggest passions are musicianship (I'm a guitarist, a singer and a songwriter), working in music production, working out, cooking and, adding the latest, writing code and learning about Web Development and online marketing.

What do I do?

My current occupation is retail salesman at a guitar shop in Ljubljana, called BAM Guitar Center, where I'm also in charge of website editing and, as of the beginning of the year 2023 I took the role of the branch manager.

As a side hustle I offer aduio production services to local musicians, which includes recording audio, editing, mixing and mastering, whilst guiding my clients through the production process and giving them valuable advice, when needed.

What do I have planned for the future?

I'm learning web development with a particular purpose, to expand my knowledge and find a job in the field of web developkment, website editing or online marketing. I'm certain I can somehow merge my experties in multimedia product creation, audio production and the newly acquired web development skills to offer a somewhat unique and holesome input to a hiring firm or future clients.

My top interests are:

  • Website development, SEO and Analytics
